Welcome to Studio Ghibli

A world where dreams take flight and imagination knows no bounds

Studio Ghibli is a world-renowned animation studio based in Tokyo, Japan, beloved for its breathtaking hand-drawn films, heartfelt storytelling, and iconic characters. Founded in 1985 by Hayao Miyazaki and Isao Takahata, the studio has created timeless works that continue to inspire audiences of all ages.

From the mysterious forests of My Neighbor Totoro to the enchanted bathhouse in Spirited Away, every Studio Ghibli film invites you to see the world through new eyes. Our stories celebrate nature, friendship, courage, and the beauty of the everyday.

This website is your gateway to explore the magic of Studio Ghibli — discover our films and series, meet unforgettable characters, stream beloved soundtracks, and stay updated with our latest news and releases.

Thank you for visiting. Let the magic continue.
